**一、病毒宇宙推荐结构比例（****Cowboy Bebop****式）**

如果把整个系列看成 **100%****素材池**，比较稳定的比例是：

|   |   |   |   |
|---|---|---|---|
|**层级**|**内容**|**比例**|**作用**|
|**Case Episodes**|单个梦境/幻觉世界|**45%**|每一集独立故事|
|**Character Arc**|Alice、J、Peter关系|**25%**|情感驱动|
|**World Mystery**|病毒规则与实验|**20%**|世界观逐渐揭露|
|**Poetic Interludes**|诗性场景、梦镜头|**10%**|气质与主题|

- **45%** **单集故事** → 保持阅读节奏
- **25%** **人物关系** → 这是读者真正关心的
- **20%** **世界观** → 太多会变百科全书
- **10%** **诗性段落** → 保持你的风格但不过载

**I. Recommended Structural Ratio for the Virus Universe (Cowboy Bebop Style)**

If the entire series is treated as a **100% material pool**, a relatively stable proportion would look like this:

| **Layer** | **Content** | **Proportion** | **Function** |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Case Episodes** | Individual dreams / illusion worlds | **45%** | Each episode functions as a standalone story |
| **Character Arc** | Relationships between Alice, J, and Peter | **25%** | Emotional driving force |
| **World Mystery** | Virus rules and the experiment | **20%** | Gradual revelation of the worldbuilding |
| **Poetic Interludes** | Poetic scenes and dream-like imagery | **10%** | Tone, atmosphere, and thematic depth |

- **45% episodic stories** → maintains narrative rhythm  
- **25% character relationships** → what readers truly care about  
- **20% worldbuilding** → too much turns the story into an encyclopedia  
- **10% poetic passages** → preserves your style without overloading the narrative
