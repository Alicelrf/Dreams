## 红毛猩猩的梦
梦见我们这个世界所有的人其实都是生活在虚拟现实里的黑猩猩和红毛猩猩(我)。是真正的人类作弄我们干的，从小就训练我们成长为人，然后让我们在虚拟现实里面以为自己真的是人以此研究他们的社会学。梦里我摘掉整个虚拟现实的设备，平静地捋了捋自己的一身红毛，想着原来如此，这个世界乱糟糟的果然都是一帮猩猩干的。
I dreamed that everyone in our world was actually chimpanzees and orangutans living inside a virtual reality simulation. I was an orangutan. Real humans had set the whole thing up as a kind of experiment. From the time we were young, they trained us to grow up “as humans,” then put us into VR so we’d believe we really were human—just so they could study their sociology.

In the dream I took off the entire VR headset and calmly brushed my long orange fur back into place. I thought, _Ah, so that’s what’s going on._

No wonder the world is such a mess—turns out it’s just a bunch of apes running around.
